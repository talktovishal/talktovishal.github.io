Vishal Chowdhary

I am an AI engineering and science leader at Microsoft, currently working on Copilot and Microsoft 365 AI.

My work has focused on language technology, machine learning, and production cloud services. I previously led work across Microsoft Translator and Azure Translator, including model quality, training and data pipelines, service infrastructure, APIs, and product experiences.

I am interested in building AI systems that connect research progress with useful, reliable products. I also enjoy explaining the ideas behind these systems through small hands-on projects.
